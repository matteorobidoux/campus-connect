Physical Education

Important Information

The following courses are required Physical Education courses: 109-101-MQ, 109-102-MQ, 109-103-MQ.

**  These courses cannot be used as complementary courses - no exceptions.  **  For complementary Physical Education courses see the complementary section of the timetable.

Students with medical problems who require placement in an appropriate course must contact either Richard Montreuil (local 4753), Layal Nakhle (local 4936), or Christine Bustamante (local 1194), prior to course allocation.

Attendance in Physical Education classes is compulsory.  Students must be dressed to participate for the first class.

Four hours of absence, for any reason, is the maximum allowed for Physical Education courses, except Outdoor Education where 100% attendance is required.

Students must complete both 109-101-MQ and 109-102-MQ before 109-103-MQ.

All 109-101-MQ sections are titled “Physical Activity and Health"; 109-102-MQ sections are titled “Physical Activity and Effectiveness"; and 109-103-MQ sections are titled “Physical Activity and Autonomy”.  Please take note of the subtitle as well as the course number when registering.

Intensive Courses
All intensive courses have one class scheduled prior to course change.  Verify lecture time and location.  All classes are compulsory.

Showering and Changing Time
Students should allow ½ hour after scheduled classes for showering and changing.
